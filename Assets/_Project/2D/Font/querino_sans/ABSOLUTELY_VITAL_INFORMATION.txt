Querino Sans is an extra-bold sans font, created by Måns Grebäck during 2018 and 2019.

It comes as a regular, upright version and as italic. 
Also check out its sister typeface Querino Script. 

The font is multilingual and has an extensive range of glyphs; it supports all Latin-based
European languages, contains numbers as well as all symbols and characters you'll ever need.
